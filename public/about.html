About Me

"I've always been fascinated by everything that can be traced back to the artistic, especially video games and music, and I've always liked the idea of giving shape to what I imagine. For the past five years, I have started to try my hand at creating both video games and music, which are the mediums through which I try to express myself. For now, I spend my time into these activities for personal pleasure, and currently I aspire to turn my passion for game development into a profession."
